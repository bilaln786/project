Our Specialized Care Programs

Comprehensive therapy services designed to support movement, communication, and hearing.

Doctor of Physical Therapy (DPT)

  • Orthopedic Rehabilitation: Personalized therapy programs for joint injuries, post-surgical recovery, and musculoskeletal disorders.

  • Sports Injury Management: Evidence-based treatment focused on injury prevention, recovery, and performance optimization.

  • Pain Management: Advanced therapeutic techniques to reduce chronic pain and restore functional movement.

Speech-Language Pathology (SLP)

  • Speech & Language Therapy: Targeted therapy to improve articulation, language development, and communication skills.

  • Voice Therapy: Specialized treatment for voice disorders caused by misuse, injury, or neurological conditions.

  • Swallowing Disorders: Clinical assessment and therapy for dysphagia to ensure safe and effective swallowing.

Audiology (AUD)

  • Hearing Assessments: Comprehensive diagnostic testing to evaluate hearing sensitivity and auditory function.

  • Hearing Aid Services: Selection, fitting, and maintenance of advanced digital hearing aids.

  • Balance & Vestibular Care: Evaluation and therapy for dizziness, balance disorders, and inner ear conditions.
